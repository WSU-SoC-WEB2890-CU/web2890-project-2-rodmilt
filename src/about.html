Classic Fencing Inc.

Fence Installation and Supply

Classic Fencing is local business serving Northern Utah's fencing needs for over 50 years. With it being a family business, each of the five Rodriguez sons have joined their father in providing service at some point.

We were established as a business in 1996 and pride ourselves, as well as, have a reputation of providing quality service above all!

In our many years, we have many repeat customers which speaks to the service we provide.
